<Warning>
  **Status: deferred.** This doc describes a **planned** Play Store distribution flow. It is **not** the current path. Dev and beta builds are distributed today via **Firebase App Distribution** — see [CI/CD Overview](/ci-cd/overview) and [Firebase App Distribution](/ci-cd/firebase-distribution).
</Warning>

How the **Internal testing** track in Google Play Console would be configured, how testers would get onboarded, how builds would flow from CI to testers' devices, and what to do when something breaks.

This document covers both Xicar Play Console apps (when Play upload is wired):

- **Prod:** `com.xicartaxi.xicar_user` — would receive AABs from the `prod` CI build
- **Beta:** `com.xicartaxi.xicar_user.beta` — would receive AABs from the `beta` CI build via **Internal app sharing**

Both apps use the **same upload keystore** and the **same Firebase project** (`xicarv2`).

---

## Why Internal testing?

| Track | Audience | Distribution | Speed |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Internal testing** | Up to 100 invited testers | Play Store app on invited accounts | Builds available within minutes; no Play Store review |
| **Closed testing** | Larger opt-in tester pool | Play Store listing, opt-in URL | No review |
| **Open testing** | Public beta users | Public Play Store listing | No review |
| **Production** | All users | Public Play Store listing | Full Play Store review |

**Internal testing** is the right fit for an internal QA loop: no Play Store review, testers get updates the moment CI uploads, up to 100 testers.

---

## Planned CI workflow

When wired, the workflow would on every `push` to `main` / `prod` and on `workflow_dispatch`:

1. Build a signed AAB
2. Upload to Play Console Internal testing track
3. Notify testers

Trigger via GitHub → **Actions** → **Build Android (AAB \+ Play Internal)** → **Run workflow**.

---

## Prerequisites (when enabled)

- Play Console app created for each flavor
- Play App Signing enrolled (already done)
- `GOOGLE_PLAY_SERVICE_ACCOUNT_JSON` secret configured
- Upload keystore in CI secrets — see [Android Keystore](/ci-cd/android-keystore)
